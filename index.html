About Ahmed Dairy Farm

Established in 1985, Fresh Valley Dairy Farm has been committed to producing the highest quality dairy products for over three decades. Our farm is nestled in the lush green valleys, providing the perfect environment for our cows to graze freely.

We believe in sustainable and ethical farming practices. Our cows are treated with care and respect, fed with organic feed, and allowed to roam freely in open pastures. This results in milk that is rich in nutrients and flavor.

From farm to table, we ensure that our products maintain their freshness and nutritional value. We use minimal processing techniques to preserve the natural goodness of our dairy products.
